Welcome to Vita AI

Thank you for using our app! Below are some common questions and support details:

How to Use

No account registration is required. When first opened, the app automatically generates a unique App ID, stored on your device and synced securely to our server.

Privacy & Data Security

Your App ID contains no personal information. Data is used solely to support app functionality and is never used for advertising or commercial purposes.

FAQ

How do I contact support?

Please tap the “Contact Support” button within the app. Our team will respond as soon as possible.

Do I need a subscription?

All features are currently free to use. No subscription is required.

What if I lose my data?

The App ID is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app will remove your data, so please back up important information when necessary.
